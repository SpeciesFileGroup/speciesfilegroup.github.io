Community Consults

Each Wednesday ask and collectively help to solve TaxonWorks (and related!) questions

These unconference-style sessions are on Wednesdays at the same time, every week. All are welcome, regardless of whether you consider yourself a collaborator. The flexible agendas are set by those attending the meetings. We use a shared Google document to record our minutes and plan our session. Adding your issues prior to the meeting helps everyone to pre-plan strategies for tackling those issues.

TaxonWorks Together

SFG's annual biodiversity informatics conference

With over 250 registrants a year TaxonWorks Together brings our community together to share what excites us about biodiversity informatics. Join us online for our 7th annual event in early May (dates coming soon).

TaxonWorks Late Nite

Once in a blue moon

Once in a while we host a "late-nite" meeting for our overseas collaboration who are in chronologically-challenged time-zones. These are chat-and-chill style. If you're interested in seeing one happen reach out.
